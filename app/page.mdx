## Description

ATRIA is a spatial computing platform built for healthcare professionals to
support collaborative pre-operative meetings and surgical preparation workflows
using Apple Vision Pro.

The app enables clinicians to securely visualize and discuss medical imaging,
surgical documentation, and clinical resources within an immersive shared
environment designed to improve communication, focus, and collaborative
decision-making.

ATRIA supports the visualization of:

- DICOM medical imaging files
- CT and MRI scans
- Ultrasound and echocardiography data
- Clinical PDFs and surgical documentation
- Surgical planning annotations

Using spatial computing technologies, healthcare teams can review patient
information together in real time through immersive collaborative sessions
integrated with Apple FaceTime.

## Features

<FeatureGrid>
  <Feature title="Meeting">
    Start a secure session in one click and invite colleagues to review a case
    together — video, voice, and screen all in one room.
  </Feature>
  <Feature title="2D / 3D Drawing">
    Sketch directly on slices or volumes with pressure-aware tools designed for
    clinical accuracy.
  </Feature>
  <Feature title="Annotation">
    Add labels, measurements, and structured notes that travel with the case and
    stay linked to the exact view.
  </Feature>
  <Feature title="DICOM Visualizer">
    Open studies straight from PACS or local files with windowing, MPR, and series
    navigation built in.
  </Feature>
  <Feature title="Shared Window">
    Keep every participant on the same slice, zoom, and pan — no more
    "scroll down a bit more" over the phone.
  </Feature>
</FeatureGrid>

## Disclaimer

ATRIA is intended only as a collaborative visualization and clinical support
tool for healthcare professionals.

The application does not provide medical diagnoses, treatment recommendations,
or autonomous clinical decision-making and does not replace professional medical
judgment.
